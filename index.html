How To Play?

Welcome to this Snakes and Ladders game! How you play this game is no different from the rest.
The red chip is you, while the blue chip is the computer.
All you have to do is click on the dices on the left to roll the dice and your chip will then move accordingly.
If you land on a ladder, you will move up board and if you land on a snake, you move down the board.
The first chip to reach 100 wins!
And most importantly, don't forget to have fun :D
